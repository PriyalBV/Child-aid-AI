Title: Family-Based Care Program – SOS Children’s Villages
Tags:Orphaned,abandoned,need of family care,adoption ,age:0–24 
Eligibility:
- Orphaned or abandoned children in need of long-term family care

Benefits:
- Children are placed in SOS families with a trained SOS Mother
- Lifelong emotional care, schooling, medical attention, and social integration
- Education till higher studies or vocational training

How to Apply:
- Children referred by CWC or local administration
- NGO evaluates suitability and space availability

Location: 22 locations in India (Delhi, Pune, Bhopal, Kolkata, etc.)

Applicable Age Group: 0–24 years
