Title: Foster Care for Orphaned Children
Tags: foster care, orphan support, child welfare, non-institutional care, CWC, ICPS, NCPCR, DCPU, financial support, family-based care, age:0-18, education, healthcare, counseling

Eligibility:
- Children without parental care and not legally adopted
- Children must be declared legally free for foster care by CWC

Benefits:
- Foster family receives ₹2,000–₹4,000 per child/month
- Education and medical expenses covered separately
- Periodic monitoring and counseling support

How to Apply:
- Apply through District Child Protection Unit
- Screening and home study of foster parents mandatory

Location: Nationwide (ICPS/NCPCR facilitated)

Applicable Age Group: 0–18 years
